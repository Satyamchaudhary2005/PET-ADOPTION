Our Adoption Process

  1. Step 1: Application

    Fill out an adoption application form. This helps us understand your preferences and lifestyle.

  2. Step 2: Meet & Greet

    Once your application is approved, you will be invited to meet the pet you are interested in.

  3. Step 3: Home Visit

    A home visit may be conducted to ensure a safe environment for the pet.

  4. Step 4: Finalization

    After the home visit, if everything is approved, you can finalize the adoption and take your new friend home!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the adoption fee?

The adoption fee varies depending on the pet. It typically covers vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and microchipping.

Can I adopt if I have other pets?

Yes! We encourage families with other pets to adopt. A meet-and-greet can help ensure compatibility.

What if I need to return the pet?

We understand that circumstances can change. If you need to return the pet, please contact us to discuss options.
