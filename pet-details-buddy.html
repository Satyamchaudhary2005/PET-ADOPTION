Frequently Asked Questions about Buddy

What is Buddy's energy level?
Buddy is a high-energy Labrador who needs regular exercise. He enjoys long walks, playing fetch, and swimming. Daily exercise of at least 1-2 hours is recommended.
Is Buddy good with children?
Yes, Buddy is very good with children! He's gentle and patient, making him an excellent family dog. However, as with any dog, supervision is recommended with very young children.
Does Buddy get along with other pets?
Buddy is friendly with other dogs and pets. He's been socialized well and enjoys playing with other dogs at the shelter.
Is Buddy house-trained?
Yes, Buddy is fully house-trained and knows basic commands like sit, stay, and come. He's also crate-trained.
What kind of home would be best for Buddy?
Buddy would do best in an active home with a yard where he can run and play. He loves being part of family activities and would thrive in a home where he gets plenty of attention and exercise.
