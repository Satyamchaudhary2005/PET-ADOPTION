Frequently Asked Questions about Lucy

What is Lucy's personality like?
Lucy is an intelligent and elegant poodle who loves learning new tricks. She's very smart, affectionate, and enjoys being the center of attention. She's great for allergy sufferers due to her hypoallergenic coat.
Is Lucy good with children and other pets?
Yes, Lucy is friendly and gentle with children and other pets. She's well-socialized and enjoys being part of a family environment.
Is Lucy house-trained?
Yes, Lucy is fully house-trained and crate-trained. She knows basic commands and walks well on a leash.
Does Lucy need a lot of grooming?
Yes, as a poodle, Lucy needs regular grooming to maintain her coat. She enjoys being groomed and will need professional grooming every 4-6 weeks, along with regular brushing at home.
What kind of home would be best for Lucy?
Lucy would thrive in an active home that can provide mental stimulation and regular exercise. She's smart and needs activities to keep her mind engaged. She would do well in both houses and apartments as long as she gets enough exercise and attention.
