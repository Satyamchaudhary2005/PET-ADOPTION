Frequently Asked Questions about Max

What is Max's personality like?
Max is a happy-go-lucky beagle with a great nose and friendly personality. He loves following scents and enjoys being active. He's very social and loves attention.
Is Max good with children and other pets?
Yes, Max is friendly with children and other pets. However, due to his strong hunting instincts, he should be supervised around small animals.
Is Max house-trained?
Yes, Max is house-trained and knows basic commands. However, he may get vocal when excited or when he smells something interesting.
Does Max need a lot of exercise?
Yes, Max needs regular exercise and mental stimulation. He loves walks and would make a great walking companion. His strong sense of smell means he'll enjoy sniffing and exploring during walks.
What kind of home would be best for Max?
Max would do best in an active home with a secure yard. He needs owners who can provide regular exercise and mental stimulation. Due to his vocal nature, he might not be suitable for apartment living.
