Frequently Asked Questions about Oliver

What is Oliver's personality like?
Oliver is a handsome tabby with a playful and independent personality. He enjoys chasing toys, watching birds from the window, and has a natural hunting instinct. He's affectionate but also values his independence.
Is Oliver good with children and other pets?
Oliver gets along well with other cats and is generally good with children who understand how to interact gently with cats. He would do best in a home with older children who respect his space.
Is Oliver litter-trained?
Yes, Oliver is fully litter-trained and very clean. He's particular about his litter box and keeps himself well-groomed.
Does Oliver need a lot of attention?
Oliver enjoys attention but is also independent. He'll seek out affection when he wants it but is happy to entertain himself with toys or by watching birds. He would do well in a home where people are present but not constantly interacting with him.
What kind of home would be best for Oliver?
Oliver would thrive in a home with plenty of vertical space to climb and explore. He enjoys having window perches and high places to observe his surroundings. A home with other cats would be fine, but he also does well as the only cat.
