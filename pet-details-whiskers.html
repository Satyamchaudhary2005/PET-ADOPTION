Frequently Asked Questions about Whiskers

What is Whiskers' personality like?
Whiskers is a gentle and affectionate cat who loves curling up in laps and chasing toy mice. She's very clean and well-mannered, making her an ideal companion for a quiet household.
Is Whiskers good with children?
Whiskers prefers a calm environment and would do best in a home without small children. She's better suited for older children who understand how to interact gently with cats.
Does Whiskers get along with other pets?
Whiskers is an indoor-only cat and would prefer to be the only pet in the household. She's not aggressive but enjoys having her own space.
Is Whiskers litter-trained?
Yes, Whiskers is fully litter-trained and very clean. She's particular about her litter box and keeps herself well-groomed.
What kind of home would be best for Whiskers?
Whiskers would thrive in a quiet, adult household where she can receive plenty of attention. She enjoys having cozy spots to nap and would appreciate a home with comfortable furniture and window perches.
